The Challenge

Africa's digital economy is expanding rapidly, yet its participation in frontier AI remains constrained by critical barriers.

Data Sovereignty

Nigeria's NDPR restricts data export, yet most AI inference occurs offshore.

Compute Gap

Less than 1% of Africa's AI workloads are processed locally due to limited GPU infrastructure.

High Latency

Average round-trip latency to U.S./EU clusters exceeds 400ms, hindering real-time services.

Ecosystem Exclusion

Developers lack compliant local access to large-scale models like GPT, Whisper, and Llama 2.

Our Solution

The Sovereign AI Infrastructure Hub integrates GPU compute, compliance, and developer services within a single platform.

DGX/H100 GPU Cluster

High-performance infrastructure for inference, fine-tuning, and edge AI workloads powered by NVIDIA technology.

AI API Proxy Gateway

Routes model requests through a local compliance layer enforcing NDPR standards.

AI Compliance Engine

Automated auditing, anonymization, and residency monitoring for full regulatory compliance.

Localized Data Pipelines

Sector-specific datasets for finance, health, agriculture, and energy tailored to African contexts.

Developer Console

SDKs and APIs for training, deployment, and model evaluation accessible to regional developers.

Low-Latency Performance

>40% reduction in latency enabling real-time AI applications and services across the region.
